My favourite Gluten Free recipes

Delightful dairy and gluten free cake that is simple, moist and fragrant. Inspired by the flavours of the Middle East this cake is both beautifully light and delightfully simple to make
I have made this cake countless times and it always goes down a treat. The rich texture comes from the olive oil that is whipped before being mixed in with the almonds. Cook in a square tray for 5 minutes less than the recommended time and you've got a tray of the most fabulous dairy and gluten free fudgey brownies. Perfect with some coconut milk ice cream!
This cake is a great alternative to the tradtional lemon drizzle. Baking with polenta is always so much fun, and the dense, buttery texture of this cake contrasted with the sharpness of the lemon makes it a sunday favourite(especially for my dad!)
